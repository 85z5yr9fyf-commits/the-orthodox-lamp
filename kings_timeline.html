The Hanging ChurchCoptic Orthodox · Cairo

The whole span of the united monarchy on one line — from the birth of Samuel, the prophet who anointed kings, through the tragic reign of Saul, the shepherd-kingship of David, and the golden age and fall of Solomon, to the day the kingdom tore in two at Shechem. Every event of 1 & 2 Samuel, 1 Kings 1–12 and 1 Chronicles is here. Dates follow the traditional chronology (Saul c. 1050–1010, David 1010–970, Solomon 970–931 BC) and most are approximate — Scripture gives sequence more often than year.
